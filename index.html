ℹ️ ABOUT

FIFA World Cup 2026

The 23rd FIFA World Cup will be held across the United States, Canada and Mexico — the first-ever edition hosted by three countries.

For the first time, 48 national teams compete across 104 matches over 39 days. The format replaces the 32-team structure used since 1998.

Opens at Estadio Azteca, Mexico City on June 11, 2026. Final at MetLife Stadium, NJ on July 19, 2026.

Opening: June 11, 2026 · Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
Final: July 19, 2026 · MetLife Stadium, New Jersey
Defending Champions: 🇦🇷 Argentina (Qatar 2022)
Most WC Titles: 🇧🇷 Brazil — 5 titles (1958,62,70,94,2002)
Teams: 48 — expanded from 32 in Qatar 2022
Prize Fund: $1 Billion USD — a world record
